FAQs Regarding DJ

Often people are unsure of what to ask a DJ they are thinking of hiring. This makes planning an event much harder for everyone. So, we want to offer you some questions to ask a potential DJ so you can be sure you choose the best event DJ.





**What kind of events do we DJ?**

You want to be sure that your DJ has experience with many different kinds of people and events, not just one. This allows them to be expert at handling difficult scenarios should they arise.





**How do we get a crowd excited?**

Do they get the party going easily and in a way that works for your event? If you are looking for a certain feel,then your DJ needs to be able to meet that while keeping the guests engaged.





**Do we have a list of available songs?**

Looking through a list of songs the DJ can play will help you choose what you want and don’t want played at your event ahead of time.If you have a specific song for one of your special dances, it is best to check if the DJ is able to get the song you need.





**What other services do we offer?**

Lots of event DJs offer other services with their event packages. Some have special lighting. Some offer HD video screens for fun effects. Knowing what the DJ offers can help make your event one-of-a-kind. Complete Weddings + Events DJs offer dance floor lighting and optional elements like Complete Interactive to create a unique experience at your event!
